About Us

ProgrammersPedia is a website aimed to improvize programming skills, knowledge and techniques of various levels of programmers by collecting study materials all together at one place and saving as much as time possible.

Our Mission

The main aim of ProgrammersPedia is to maximize India's contribution in the field of programming which has been lacking from last few decades.

Our Plan

Our plan is to take programming skills of everyone to next level. But make it free while we can and help content reach to maximum people of country.
